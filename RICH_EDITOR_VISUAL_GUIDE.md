## Keyboard Shortcuts

- **Bold**: Ctrl/Cmd + B
- **Italic**: Ctrl/Cmd + I
- **Link Dialog**: Click link button, Enter to insert
- **Image Dialog**: Click image button, Enter to insert
- **Undo**: Ctrl/Cmd + Z
- **Redo**: Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + Z or Ctrl/Cmd + Y
- **Tab Navigation**: Tab through toolbar buttons
- **Dialog Escape**: Esc to close dialogs
